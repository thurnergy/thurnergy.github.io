About Thurnergy

Thunergy is a startup energy company planning to provide clean renewable energy throughout the Republic of South Sudan through off-the-grid sources of energy. Endowed with vast renewables, South Sudan has a potential for sustainable energy and Thunergy will tap into those resources. Many regions of the country receive an average of 200 hours of sunlight per month a year. Not only is there an abundance of solar resources, there is substantial potential for hydroelectric power and bio-energy. Despites the abundance, little of it is being used. The entire country has little access to electricity. Even the capital city, Juba, gets its energy from generation - a source which is environmentally unfriendly as well as unsustainable. Thunergy is gearing up to bridge that gap. Thunergy targets both urban and rural South Sudanese. Focus, however, lies on providing electricity to rural communities.

Thurnergy CEO

What we do

Services Provision Strategy

Installation of Solar Unit for Free, after which customer pays fixed monthly electricity bill. Such trade will only be based on a trust between the company and its customers. To achieve smooth trade, the company will ask prospective customer to provide evidence of the source of income as a security. These may include bank statement, authentic formal letter from employee indicating monthly salary, or prove of business ownership.

Targeting

Thunergy will work collaboratively with local administrators to ensure sustainable availability of electricity. The company will ask a chief or commissioner to vouch for any prospective customer. This will give the company confidence to install a unit, as the administrator will be liable to cost should the customer fail to pay. If the administrators are reluctant to take responsibility, but at least one existing customer can vouch, then Thunergy may install a unit.

Installation

The company will not only provide a rooftop solar panel for an individual, it can also install a communal unit for communities that have organized them. This will be commonly done in rural areas where individual family will not be able to afford the cost independently. Thunergy will also work on contract with government institutions, International Non-governmental Organization, Corporations, as well as households.

Bridging the Gap

Thurnergy will work with local governments to install solar panels in their provinces.

Thurnergy will work with citizens to encourage alternative energy sources in their private spaces.

We aim to modernize and revolutionize the way South Sudan is powered.
